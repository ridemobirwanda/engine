Step 1: Add Your Video File

Place your video file in the public/videos/ directory and name it engine-background.mp4

public/videos/engine-background.mp4

Step 2: Video Requirements

  • Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
  • Duration: 10-30 seconds (will loop automatically)
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
  • File Size: Keep under 10MB for best performance
  • Content: Engine/automotive related footage works best

Step 3: Alternative Video Sources

If you don't have a video file, you can use these free sources:

  • Pixabay - Engine Videos
  • Pexels - Free Videos
  • Unsplash - Free Videos

Step 4: Test Your Video

After adding your video file, visit:

http://localhost:8081/

or

http://localhost:8082/

Step 5: Fallback Animation

If no video is found, the page will show a beautiful animated gradient background with floating particles instead.
